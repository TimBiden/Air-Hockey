• The game is won when either the computer or player reach 11 points.
  • Press the Spacebar to drop the puck.
  • Use your arrow keys to move your paddle up and down.
  • Move the volume slider to add sound.
  • Easy: Puck moves slowly and the computer has a 1:3 chance of missing the puck.
  • Medium: Puck moves at a medium pace and the computer has a 1:6 chance of missing the puck.
  • Hard: Puck moves quickly and the computer has a 1:10 chance of missing the puck.
  • Default setting: Medium chance of missing the puck.
